How Can We Help?

Wine Discovery

New to Georgian wine? Begin your journey with the Château Mukhrani Saperavi Reserve — our most recommended wine for first-time Georgian wine explorers. Looking for Georgian wine online? Browse our collection to find your perfect bottle. Our team is happy to provide further personalised recommendations.

Winery Visits

Planning a trip to Georgia's wine country? We can help you arrange visits to Château Mukhrani in Mtskheta or Shumi Winery in Kakheti. Get in touch for personalised recommendations and introductions.

Press & Partnerships

Wine writers, sommeliers, importers, and trade professionals — we welcome collaboration. Select the "Partnership & Press" subject in the form above and our team will respond within one business day.

Cellar Society

Interested in our newsletter and exclusive Cellar Society updates? Scroll to the bottom of our homepage to subscribe, or select "Cellar Society" in the contact form subject line.
