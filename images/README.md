## Recommended Specs
- **Format:** JPG (preferred) or WebP
- **Hero/Background images:** at least 1600px wide
- **Winery/estate photos:** at least 1200px wide, 16:9 ratio works best
- **Wine bottle photos:** portrait orientation (3:4 ratio), at least 800px tall, ideally on a clean or neutral background
- **Other photos:** at least 1000px on the longest side

## Notes
- File names are **case-sensitive** — use lowercase exactly as listed
- The website already has proper `alt` text on every image for SEO and accessibility
- If a photo is missing, the slot will just appear as an empty space — no error shown to users
